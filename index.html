About

Plunderlings aims to present a fresh take on fantasy universes from a Caribbean perspective. A place where the “monsters” are pirate adventurers with emotions, hopes and dreams of their own. Plunderlings will continue expand this new world to incorporate new creatures inspired from island animals, legends and history.

Lone Coconut is based in the Dominican Republic. ​
